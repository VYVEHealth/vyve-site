Professional Services 250 Employees 8-Week Pilot
The Challenge

Spending on wellbeing. Seeing nothing back.

The HR Director had a problem she couldn't ignore. Absence had risen to 11.2 days per employee — above the UK average. An EAP was in place, but fewer than 4% of employees had ever used it. A gym subsidy was available, but take-up sat below 15%.

The core issue: every existing benefit was reactive or passive. Nobody was proactively supporting employees day-to-day, and there was no data to tell the HR team who was struggling before it became an absence event.

What We Did

8 weeks. Full programme. Real results.

W1

Onboarding & activation

All 250 employees invited. Kahunas app installed, initial health questionnaire completed. 61% activated within 7 days — well above industry benchmark for corporate wellness launches.

W2

First live sessions

Daily habit tracking went live. The first cohort of live sessions ran — yoga, workouts, and a weekly mindfulness check-in. 89 employees attended their first live session in week two.

W4

Mid-point pulse

Wellbeing score: 6.8/10 — up from a baseline of 5.4. Daily habit completion rate hit 73% among active members. First 30-habit certificates issued.

W8

Results & renewal

Programme review with the HR Director and CFO. Results presented. 12-month contract signed within two weeks of the review meeting.

"We've tried gym subsidies, an EAP, even a mindfulness app. VYVE is the first thing where people are actually using it — and we can see exactly who's engaging and who we need to reach. The data alone is worth the investment."
